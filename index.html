Who is Techome Management?

TecHome Management is a full-service property management company based in Port Andratx. Our primary focus is on providing concierge level of service for our clients. Our company directors have experience in the elite level security, systems integration, residential property development and hospitality industries, our expertise in these areas in combination with our laser focus on providing the highest level of customer service, results in a level of care and efficiency that will be found nowhere else in Mallorca. We offer a full range of property maintenance services and create a custom package that wo rks specifically for you, and your property.

Trust, and confidentiality is at the forefront of our business and we understand how important it is as a property owner to be able to leave your property in the hands of someone you can, not only rely upon, but that leaves you feeling completely at ease knowing that TecHome Management has every aspect of your property under control.

At TecHome Management we make ourselves available around the clock to give you total peace of mind and to care for your property. In addition to our property management services, our exclusive concierge and guest services are available to all of our clients. We have partnered with many local and international companies to offer a wide range of convenient services.
